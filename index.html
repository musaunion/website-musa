Mansha'ul Uloom is part of a larger moral initiative established before the 1920s. In the heart of historic Omanur in Cheekode Panchayat of Malappuram district, the great QADIMUL ISLAM ASHAIQ MUHAMMED SWABOOR IBN KALANTHARUL HAMADANI (RA), a prominent Sufi and scholar and the link of Suhrawardi Tawriqat, has waqf the place and started this initiative. Possessing many karamats, she built mosques and mahallaras in many places by training her disciples in the perfect deen system. For more than half a century, Dars had been held in the said institution, which had been suggested by the great Bafaqi Thangal Manshaul Uloom, under the century-old Omanur Tarbiyyatul Muslimin Sangha. Manshaul Uloom is a great institution where many scholars including eminent Sufis Waliullahi Vandoor Ustad (RA) studied Ilm. Today many educational and spiritual projects are going forward.


MANSHA'UL ULOOM SHUHADA ACADEMY

It is functioning as an affiliated institution of SNEC, the national educational scheme of Samasta, in the name of historical Omanur Shuhada. After SSLC the opportunity to study in religious physical education in a more productive manner with excellent teachers and systems. Arabic, Urdu and English lessons are also provided. Along with SNEC studies, hafilims are also given Daura under the guidance of Hafil Ustads. Excellent hostel facilities and food are provided. With the help of generous donations, education is now provided entirely free of cost.
